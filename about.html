Capture The Flags

  •  > Capture the Flag (CTF) is a special kind of information security competitions.
  •  > CTF stands for Capture The Flag, a style of hacking event where you have one goal: hack in and find the flag.
  •  > Flags are placed in various locations - your goal is to hunt them all down.
